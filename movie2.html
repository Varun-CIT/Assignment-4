The Shore Temple

  • Shore Temple, Mahabalipuram-where Dravidian craftsmanship meets divine legacy.
  • A sacred stone marvel, echoing the artistic brilliance and spiritual depth of Pallava heritage.
  • Here, history and devotion stand eternal against the sea.
  • Construction: 725 AD
  • Location : Mahabalipuram,TamilNadu
  • Historical role : Served as a landmark for seafarers and was part of a busy port
