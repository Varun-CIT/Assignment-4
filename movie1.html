Taj Mahal

  • A jewel of Mughal splendor, the Taj Mahal was built by Shah Jahan in honor of his queen, Mumtaz Mahal.
  • Its white-marble grace and timeless beauty make it an eternal symbol of love..
  • Location : Agra,India
  • Construction year:1632-1653 AD
  • Key Features:Central dome, minarets, intricate carvings
